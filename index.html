ABOUT EVENT

UAP Cyber Siege is the premier cybersecurity competition bringing together the
brightest minds to tackle real-world security challenges

Join us for an exciting competition that challenges participants to demonstrate their skills in various cybersecurity domains. From ethical hacking to system defense, forensics to cryptography, participants will face increasingly complex security scenarios. The competition is designed to simulate real-world cyber threats, providing valuable hands-on experience while competing for prestigious awards and recognition in the cybersecurity community.
